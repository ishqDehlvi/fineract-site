Platform for Digital Financial Services



Apache Fineract (\’fīn-,ә-,rakt\) is open source software for financial services.
At Apache Fineract(tm), our mission is to build, maintain and enhance a cloud-ready core banking system for robust, scalable, and secure operations of financial institutions. Fineract provides a reliable, robust, and affordable solution for entrepreneurs, financial institutions, and service providers. We believe in financial services for everyone, including and especially for the unbanked and underbanked. Fineract(tm) is aimed at innovative mobile and cloud-based solutions, and enables digital transaction accounts for all. Fineract(tm) 1.x is a mature platform with open APIs (see releases below), while Fineract(tm) CN (under development) is a cloud native, microservice architecture also supporting open banking APIs.


Fineract

Fineract offers a versatile platform that can be deployed in any environment and tailored to suit any organization's requirements. It provides the necessary tools for managing client data, loan and savings portfolios, real-time accounting, and financial reporting.

Learn More

Fineract CN

Fineract CN is a microservices-based platform for digital financial service providers. Each microservice encapsulates a domain that can be combined to create new platforms. Fineract CN can process over 1000 transactions/second and includes a backoffice UI. It has been incubated at Apache since 2015 and is used by a community of companies.
